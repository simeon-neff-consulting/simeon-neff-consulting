About the Business

Simeon Neff Consulting provides business and organizational consulting services to specialty food producers, with a focus on Central Coast organic olive oil companies. Leveraging expertise in Industrial & Organizational Psychology, we help producers create thriving workplaces, streamline operations, and strengthen brand identity.

Our Niche

  • Focus on high-polyphenol olive oils for rich flavor and health benefits
  • Guidance on production and storage methods that preserve polyphenols
  • Marketing strategies that educate consumers on premium olive oil value

Services

  • Organizational Consulting: Workflow analysis, leadership coaching, employee engagement programs
  • Brand & Marketing Strategy: Positioning your olive oil as a premium, health-forward product
  • Operations Support: Streamlining production while maintaining artisanal quality standards
